Informal®

Brand Identity

Informal® is our way of being, our approach to the world and the problems that we face. We believe in hungry people. People with a collaborative mind, passion, curiosity and taste. Our vision is to bring these people together to make a mark and accelerate a positive change in the industry—showing a better way to build more human food experiences. We’re Informal.

05
Typography
Download

The Informal® brand uses the typefaces Post Grotesk designed by Josh Finklea and Lyon Display designed by Kai Bernau. Post Grotesk is a contemporary version of the traditional grotesk sans-serif. The intention was to build an amiable typeface with maximum usability and an overall sense of neutrality. Lyon Display is a modern interpretation of the classic Renaissance typefaces. Lyon brings character and acts as layer of substance to our brand.
