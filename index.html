Informal®

Brand Identity

Informal® is our way of being, our approach to the world and the problems that we face. We believe in hungry people. People with a collaborative mind, passion, curiosity and taste. Our vision is to bring these people together to make a mark and accelerate a positive change in the industry—showing a better way to build more human food experiences. We’re Informal.

Brand Signature
Download

The Informal® brand signature is typed in Post Grotesk Black. It’s a homogenous and functional signature that can be applied in any format or setting. Based on it’s weight (Black) it can be either discrete or loud depending on the scale and placement of the signature.

Colors

The core palette of our brand is black, white and red. Black and white is the fundamental colors of everything we do, they are what facilitates our contents. Red is our attitude, the rebellious way of approaching the everyday—reflecting passion, strength, courage, leadership, excitement and energy.

05 D
Typography

The Informal® brand uses the typefaces Post Grotesk designed by Josh Finklea. Post Grotesk is a contemporary version of the traditional grotesk sans-serif. It brings human character to our brand while still being a functional typeface.
